How to Start Dungeons and Dragons

What is Dungeons and Dragons?

Dungeons and Dragons is a tabletop adventure game brought to life with the worlds greatest tool, the mind! With a lovely set of dice and supreme story telling and guidance from the Dungeon Master (DM), you will embark on treturous quests hunting monsters, saving NPC's and most importantly, slaying Dragons!

What roles are there?

This is a question which has been asked to me quite a fair amount and I will always say, depends on which role you take!

Woah, did you just read theres more than one role to attending a session?! Thats right, theres 2 roles.
Theres the player which will work as a part of the team in combat, exploring and problem solving and anything they can think of whilst the other is the Dungeon Master.


What the Dungeon Master does is essentially be the referee for the pod by:
•Describing Scenery, Surroundings and Narrative
•Rolling to see if said action happens
•Rolling for enemies attack and spells
•Saving Throws (spells which target character specifically)
•Levelling up characters

What makes the game fun is working with the Dungeon Master by testing the limits of the scenario but also at the same time, having a respect to the DM. As long as theres leneancy but still within reason, you'll have a blast. At the end of the day, why annoy the one person essentially playing God within your story.
